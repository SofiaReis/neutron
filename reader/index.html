How to play

Neutron

Are you ready to bring the neutron to your homerank?

How to play

  • BOARD: 5x5 board;
  • SOLDIERS: A soldier moves in a straight line (orthogonal or diagonal). It must move as far as it can; so it continues until it finds an edge. Each player has 5 soldiers (white VS black); There is no capturing.
  • NEUTRON: The neutral piece moves like the soldier;
  • TURNS: Each player first moves the neutron, then one of his soldiers; On the first turn, the first player does not move the neutron.
  • GOAL: Wins the player that gets the neutron on his first row (or homerank, where his soldiers start the game) or stalemate the opponent, so that he cannot finish his turn.
